I am a graduate student in the [Department of Computer Science](https://www.cs.purdue.edu/) at [Purdue University](https://www.purdue.edu/), where my research is supervised by [Prof. Rohan Paleja](https://www.rohanpaleja.com/) in the [SCALE Robotics Lab](https://scale-robotics-lab.github.io/index.html). 
I am grateful to the [German Academic Exchange Service (DAAD)](https://www.daad.de/en/) for generously [funding my master's studies](https://www2.daad.de/ausland/studieren/stipendium/de/70-stipendien-finden-und-bewerben/?detail=57503584) in the United States. 

***This summer***, I am at the [University of Oxford](https://www.ox.ac.uk/) for a research visit with [Prof. Ioannis Havoutis](https://ihavoutis.github.io/) in the [Dynamic Robot Systems Group](https://ori.ox.ac.uk/groups/drs-control) at the [Oxford Robotics Institute](https://ori.ox.ac.uk/).

In 2025, I completed my bachelor's degree in computer science at [Leipzig University of Applied Sciences](https://www.htwk-leipzig.de/en/htwk-leipzig), where I was honored for graduating top of my class and received multiple awards for my bachelor's thesis. As part of the degree program, I worked as a part-time software engineer at [Deutsche Telekom](https://www.telekom.com/de) for three years and wrote my thesis at [Carl Zeiss Microscopy](https://www.zeiss.com/microscopy/en/home.html) during a six-month R&D internship.

<h3 class="teaching-separator">Awards</h3>

In 2025, I was awarded a **scholarship for a master's degree in the United States** by the [German Academic Exchange Service (DAAD)](https://www.daad.de/en/). The scholarship covers tuition and living expenses at Purdue University over a two-year period.

When I graduated from Leipzig University of Applied Sciences in 2025, I received an **award for graduating top of my class** and an **award for the best bachelor's thesis**. In 2026, I also received an ***[Applied Digitalization](https://www.actemium.de/news/foerderpreis-fuer-angewandte-digitalisierung-2026/)* thesis award** from [Actemium Deutschland](https://www.actemium.com/) for my bachelor's thesis.

Before my university studies, I attended a [selective STEM-focused high school](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Carl-Zeiss-Gymnasium_Jena) in Jena, Germany. Upon graduating with distinction in 2021, I received multiple awards, including an **outstanding graduate award** from the [German Physical Society](https://www.dpg-physik.de/).

<h3 class="teaching-separator">Service</h3>

I serve as a **student co-chair** of the [Technical Committee on Robot Learning](https://ieee-robotlearning.github.io/) of the [IEEE Robotics and Automation Society](https://www.ieee-ras.org/).

<h3 class="teaching-separator">Teaching</h3>

In *Spring 2026*, I was a **graduate teaching assistant** for the course [AI Essentials: A Non-Technical Introduction (GRAD 503)](https://catalog.purdue.edu/preview_course_nopop.php?catoid=18&coid=215412) in the Department of Political Science at Purdue University.

At Leipzig University of Applied Sciences, I was an **undergraduate teaching assistant** for *three semesters (2023-2024)*, where I developed exercises for [new courses in mathematics and computer science](https://modulux.htwk-leipzig.de/app-modulux/frontend/ausgabe/studiengaenge/?no_cache=1&tx_ezqueries_ezqueriesplugin%5Bfilters%5D%5Bm_stg.m_stg_id%5D=294&tx_ezqueries_ezqueriesplugin%5Bfilters%5D%5BfilterType%5D=strict&tx_ezqueries_ezqueriesplugin%5Baction%5D=detail&tx_ezqueries_ezqueriesplugin%5Bcontroller%5D=RecordManagement) that are now part of the [degree program's curriculum](https://www.htwk-leipzig.de/studieren/studiengaenge/kooperative-studiengaenge/telekommunikationsinformatik-kooperativer-studiengang#c215690). 
Additionally, as a **research associate** for *one semester (2025)*, I contributed to [PyRope](https://pyrope-edu.readthedocs.io/index.html), a Python package that helps university instructors create digital STEM exercises with integrated assessment.
